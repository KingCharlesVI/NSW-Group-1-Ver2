Task Force-160th

1st Battalion, 160th SOAR

AFSOC Culture

TF 160th is dedicated to the precision delivery and protection of the elite forces of the United States Special Operations. Hosting multiple transport, CAS, and ISR platforms; crews of TF 160th operate at the highest levels of aviation.

Dedicated Pipeline

To join the coveted ranks of the task force, and become a Nightstalker, you must first complete all three phases of initial entry training to prepare you for the rigorous demands of aviation excellence. The three phases consist of an initial assessment flight, green platoon, and flight training.
