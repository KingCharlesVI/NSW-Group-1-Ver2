Air Force Special Operations

24th STS

AFSOC Culture

"First There". This motto summarizes the 24th STS excellency in combat controlling, managing to safely direct air traffic and coordinate air support for their attached units. Our CCT's are masters of their craft and represent the pinnacle of AFSOC, which can only be achieved through dedication to the unit and arduous training. Professionalism, accountability and dedication are our core values.

Dedicated Pipeline

After having shown their commitment to the unit in BUDS, AFSOC applicants will ingress STG OTC and learn what it takes to operate at the highest level of special operations while simultaneously performing their duties as a combat controller, thus really getting to grips with what it really means to be First there.
