About the Staff Shops

The Staff Shops are diverse and are open to each member that has been serving in the unit for greater than 3 months.
Due to the increase of administrative demands, and back-end work complications, we have determined that the best way to handle these challenges is as a team. This team of Admin Shops will be made up of 4 primary sections, each with their own sub-categories necessary to share the workload. Anyone is welcome to apply to these sections using the form found in the info-board channel. A single member may hold up to three different shop responsibilities. These are collateral duties and are not intended to interfere with your obligations to your primary role within the unit. If you are interested in helping the unit grow, please apply to one of the following sections that appeals to you:
